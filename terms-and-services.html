Terms and Services – Luma

Welcome to Luma! To ensure a relaxing and enjoyable experience for all our guests, please take a moment to review our terms and conditions carefully. By confirming your booking with us, you agree to these terms:

Reservation and Check-In

  • Check-in time is from 2:00 PM onwards.

  • Check-out time is by 11:00 AM.

  • Early check-in and late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Quiet and Peaceful Atmosphere

  • Music Policy: To maintain tranquility, playing music is strictly prohibited in all areas of the resort, including guest rooms.

  • Please respect other guests by keeping noise levels to a minimum, especially between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.

Pets Policy

  • Although we love animals, pets are not permitted at Luma. This policy helps ensure the safety and comfort of all guests, considering potential allergies, fears, and interactions with local animals.

Smoking Policy

  • For your health and the comfort of all our guests, Luma is a completely non-smoking property, including all rooms and common areas.

  • A cleaning fee may apply if evidence of smoking is found within resort premises.

Electricity Disclaimer

  • The electricity supply in South Goa can be unpredictable. Occasionally, there may be interruptions in electricity supply, affecting the huts and common areas. While we regret any inconvenience this may cause, these situations are beyond our control. We encourage guests to embrace these moments as part of their authentic holiday experience in India.

Parking Facilities

  • Complimentary parking spaces are available at Luma. Parking is provided on a first-come, first-served basis and is subject to availability.

  • Please note that the resort assumes no responsibility for loss or damage to vehicles parked on our property.

Guest Responsibilities

  • Guests are responsible for maintaining cleanliness and treating resort property with care.

  • Damages caused to property, fixtures, or fittings will incur additional charges.

Liability

  • Luma does not accept responsibility for loss or damage to personal belongings. Guests are advised to use the safes provided in rooms to store valuables securely.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations or amendments to bookings are subject to the resort’s cancellation policy, which you will receive upon booking confirmation.

Thank you for choosing Luma. We look forward to providing you with a serene and unforgettable stay!
