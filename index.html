Welcome to Luma!

Welcome to Luma, a tranquil retreat tucked away in the heart of South Goa. Our 9 A-Frame huts blend rustic charm with a touch of magic—each featuring a private open-sky bathroom for showering under the stars.

Just a short hop from the coast, we offer a peaceful escape surrounded by nature, perfect for those seeking simplicity and serenity in one of India's most beloved destinations.

Unplug, unwind, and let South Goa's magic wash over you.

Discover Palolem and Beyond

Our resort is just steps away from the picturesque Palolem Beach—known for its calm waters, stunning sunsets, and friendly atmosphere. Explore charming seaside cafés offering fresh local cuisine, lively markets filled with vibrant textiles and crafts, and abundant wellness activities, including yoga classes, kayaking, paddleboarding, and dolphin-spotting trips.
